ABOUT US


Nirankar Restaurant is the name that comes to the mind of the foodaholic people of Melbourne when they talk about pure, authentic Indian cuisine. The restaurant offers a wide array of Indian and Nepalese cuisines. Being one of the best Indian restaurants in Melbourne, the restaurant strives to always use the freshest produce, and give the best value for money. Nirankar Restaurant has been an integral part of Indian and Nepalese restaurants in Melbourne, serving the food lovers of the city with the delightful taste of pure and original Indian and Nepalese food.
